MUJ HACKS 7.0

1st October - 2nd October

Hacks 7.0

In this event. participants shall race against the clock in an intense 36-hour challenge to learn, build and improve their prototypes. It provides a launching base for many budding tech enthusiasts to go forward with their skills in design, development and pitching. It shall project the initiative to shape a community of coders as they compute through the night, the primary objective not being to compete in a themed setting but rather approach a much greater goal to bring about-a culture of driven enthusiasm, team work and learning.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the team size?

Maximum team size is four members.

What is the participation fee?

The event is free for all.

Where will we submit our projects?

This is a 36-hour Hackathon & submissions would be take on Devfolio. Anyone failing to do so before the deadline will not be allowed to contest in the Hackathon.

What if someone has copied the code from another project?

We do not tolerate any form of PLAGIARISM.
The project and the idea that you are working on should be unique and should have been made during the Hackathon
